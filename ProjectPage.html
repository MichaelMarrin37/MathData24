About the Project

This project aims to use data science techniques to identify various species of saltwater catfish from images. Accurate identification is crucial for monitoring populations and implementing conservation strategies.

Data Science Techniques

We use machine learning models trained on thousands of images to accurately identify different species of saltwater catfish. This involves advanced image recognition techniques to differentiate between species based on visual characteristics.

Why Conservation Matters

Ecological Role

Saltwater catfish play a crucial role in marine ecosystems as both predators and prey. They help maintain the balance of marine life and contribute to the health of coral reefs and other marine habitats.

Threats to Saltwater Catfish

Overfishing, habitat destruction, and climate change are significant threats to saltwater catfish populations. These threats can lead to population declines, which in turn affect the broader marine ecosystem.

How Our Project Helps

By improving the accuracy of species identification, our project provides vital data that can inform conservation strategies, ensuring that efforts are targeted and effective.
